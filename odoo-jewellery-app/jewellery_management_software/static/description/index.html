The Jewellery Management app in Odoo is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline and optimize the operations of businesses in the Jewellery industry. This app integrates with core Odoo modules such as Product, Sales Management, Purchase, and Stock to provide a unified platform for managing various aspects of Jewellery business operations. Below is a detailed description of the Jewellery Management app.

Product Management

Product Catalog

Easily create and manage an extensive catalog of Jewellery items, including detailed product descriptions, images, and specifications.

Variants and Customization

Handle different variants of Jewellery items such as size, color, material, and design customizations to meet customer preferences.

Pricelist Management

Maintain multiple price lists for different customer segments, special offers, and promotions.

Purchase Management

Supplier Management

Maintain detailed records of suppliers, including contact information, pricing agreements, and purchase history.

Reference No

This is the reference number for the main product. Each main product has unique components associated with it when purchased.

Purchase Orders

Automate the creation of purchase orders based on stock levels, sales forecasts, and supplier lead times.

Purity

This indicates the purity of the main jewelry set, such as 10KT, 14KT, 18KT, or 22KT.

Quantity 2

This field reflects the quantity, ranging from 1g to 5ct or from 1000kg to 1 ton.

UoM 2

This indicates the name of the unit of measurement (UoM){For Quantity 2}.

Piece

Product components already have two units of measurement. The third unit of measurement, "piece," is used for items like pearls, diamonds, stones, etc., which vary in size. Quantity 2: This field reflects the quantity, ranging from 1g to 5ct or from 1000kg to 1 ton.

Gross Weight

When the Quantity2 and UOM2 values of a component are 'g', add these values Total to the main product's Gross Weight Total field. This action is triggered by clicking the button labeled 'Gross Weight'.

Vendor Bills and Payments

Manage vendor bills and payments seamlessly to ensure timely settlements and maintain good supplier relationships.

Inventory and Stock Management

Stock Tracking

Real-time tracking of inventory levels across multiple locations and warehouses.

Stock Movements

Monitor stock movements including incoming shipments, internal transfers, and outgoing deliveries to ensure accurate inventory records.

Lot and Serial Number Tracking

Track batches and individual items with lot and serial numbers to ensure traceability and compliance. When the main product and its attached components are purchased (e.g. Necklace set), the main product is stored in stock, while its components are moved to a virtual location as defined by the putaway rules.

Sales Management

Quotation and Order Management

Generate professional quotations and sales orders with detailed item descriptions and pricing.

Point of Sale (POS)

Integrated POS system for efficient in-store sales and inventory updates in real-time. In the sales order page, Use the main product in the 'Main Product Name' field. The main product and all its components will automatically be retrieved from the corresponding purchase order based on their reference number. Retrieved data added to the sales order line.

Manufacturing

In our manufacturing process, there are two types: indoor and outdoor. Outdoor manufacturing involves multiple steps, including subcontracting to different jewelry shops and utilizing various locations such as work centers, vendors, or third parties. For each part of the process (Work Order or Job Card), we create distinct Bills of Materials (BOMs), such as GHAT, MEENA, JADTAR, FITTING, POLISH, etc. These BOMs are interconnected, resulting in the creation of the main product BOM. Once the Manufacturing Order (MO) for the main product is confirmed, corresponding child MOs are automatically generated.

Integration with Other Modules

Seamlessly integrates with Odoo Accounting, CRM, and Marketing modules to provide a holistic view of business operations and enhance overall efficiency.
